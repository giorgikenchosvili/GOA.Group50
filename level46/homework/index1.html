Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Use a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in eggs, tilt pan.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Sprinkle your chosen fillings.
  5. Fold and serve: Lift edge, fold, cook another minute.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot!
